Tacos

Spicy, tangy, sweet, this recipe for steak tacos brings all the flavors to the table.

Ingredients List

  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • ½ cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 3 limes, juiced
  • 4 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 teaspoons dark brown sugar/li
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 ½ teaspoons red pepper flakes
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 pound skirt steak, fat removed and flattened to 1/8-inch thickness
  • 4 corn tortillas

Steps

  1. Step 1 Combine onion, cilantro, lime juice, soy sauce, olive oil, brown sugar, oregano, red pepper flakes, and garlic in a large bowl.
  2. Step 2 Place steak in a baking pan and pour marinade on top. Cover and refrigerate at least 1 to 4 hours.
  3. Step 3 Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  4. Step 4 Grill steak 2 to 4 minutes per side, to desired doneness. Allow meat to rest 5 to 10 minutes before slicing against the grain into 1/4-inch slices./li>
  5. Step 5 Add meat to tortillas and top with your favorite toppings.
